Consignor Registration

Join hundreds of local families who consign at Duds for Love Bugs. Earn a minimum of 60% of your sales — and even more when you volunteer.

How to Register

1

Pay the $12 consignor fee

You'll complete a Consignor Agreement before payment. Registration is required for each sale.

2

Tag your items and schedule drop-off

Price and tag your items using MySaleManager, then schedule an appointment to drop them off. Bring items sorted by gender and size.

3

Shop the VIP Pre-Sale

As a consignor, you get exclusive early access on Thursday evening from 6–8 PM. Volunteers get in even earlier at 5 PM.

4

Get paid within two weeks

Receive a check for your sold items within two weeks of the sale. Pick up unsold items on Sunday from 6–7:30 PM sharp — items not picked up by 7:30 PM become DFLB property and are donated to a local charity.
