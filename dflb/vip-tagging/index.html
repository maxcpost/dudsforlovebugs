No Time to Tag? We Do ALL the Work for You

Clean out those closets, garages, and playrooms — and cash in without lifting a finger. Our VIP Tagging Service is perfect for busy families who want to earn money, save time, and shop first.

Apply for VIP/Valet Tagging

Two Ways to Use VIP Tagging

Choose the option that fits your schedule. Both include expert pricing, consignor pre-sale access, and a check within two weeks.

VIP

You Drop Off

  • Minimum 25 items, maximum 500 items
  • Top quality items only
  • $15 supply fee per 200 items (deducted from your check)

No pickup fee — you bring items to us

Valet VIP

We Pick Up

  • Minimum 50 items, maximum 500 items
  • Top quality items only
  • $15 supply fee per 200 items (deducted from your check)
  • $35 pickup service fee (due at pickup)

We come to you — zero effort on your part

How It Works

1

Submit your application

Fill out and submit the VIP Application as soon as possible. Spots are limited and available on a first come, first served basis.

2

Register to consign ($12 fee)

Once approved, register as a consignor and pay the $12 non-refundable registration fee through the Consignor Login.

3

Arrange drop-off or pickup within 7 days

You'll have 7 days to coordinate your item drop-off (VIP) or schedule a pickup at your home (Valet VIP).

4

Sit back and get paid

We handle the sorting, pricing, tagging, and displaying. Receive your check within two weeks after the sale.
