Tagging Merchandise

Everything you need to know about creating tags, pricing items, and preparing your merchandise for the sale.

Creating Your Tags

You'll enter the following information for each item in MySaleManager:

Category

Choose from the drop-down menu (e.g., girls' clothing). Leave blank if not listed.

Size

Select from the drop-down: 0-3mo through 16 (18/20 boys), plus Juniors for girls. If it reads "small," guess a number size.

Description

Brief description like "Gap turtle tee" or "Gap denim jeans." For Purple Play Rack items, include "PPRack."

Price

Minimum $1.00, in $0.50 increments. Price at 25-40% off retail. Fair pricing = higher sale rate = higher profits.

Quantity

Add multiple tags at once for identical items with the same price and description.

Discount & Donate

Discount: Check to mark 50% off on Sunday. Donate: Check to donate unsold items to the Crisis Nursery.

Important Reminders

  • Save often — the system times out after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  • Click "I'm Finished For Now" when done entering tags.
  • Use "Print Preview" to check barcodes are clear and legible before printing.
  • Print tags on 60-67 lb. card stock.

Troubleshooting: No Barcode Printing?

  1. Disable your pop-up blocker
  2. Try using Internet Explorer (or Edge in IE mode)
  3. On Mac, try scaling your tags to make them larger

How to Tag Your Items

The most important step: make sure ALL tags are SECURE.

Do NOT tape over barcodes. All items must be carefully inspected — no stains, holes, missing buttons, broken zippers, animal hair, or unpleasant odors.

Clothing

Use a tagging gun (preferred) or safety pins. Position the hanger so it looks like a question mark (?). Attach the tag in the upper right-hand corner of the garment.

Shoes

Must be odor-free, scuff-free, and cleaned top and bottom. Secure pairs together — zip-ties work best.

Toys

Must be clean, in working order, and include all parts. Battery-operated toys must have working batteries installed.

Bedding, Furniture & Baby Gear

Bedding: Clean, no stains, rips, or tears. Furniture and baby gear: Must be in "like-new" condition.

Loose Items

Socks, hats, bibs, bottles, and other small items sell best in labeled Ziploc bags.

Drop-Off

Bring Items Organized

  • Sorted by gender and size
  • Use rubber bands to group items together
  • By appointment only
