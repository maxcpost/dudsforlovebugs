## Meet the Owner

I would like to take a moment and introduce myself! My name is Natalie; I am a wife, mama of three and love shopping! I studied business at West Virginia University and dreamt of someday having my own. Inspired and affectionately named because of my very own love bugs, DFLB launched in 2016. My vision: a children's "pop-up" boutique! It's an honor to be able to offer a service where local families can not only shop, but also clean out and cash in! I like to describe it as "going green". Not only can you recycle your "gently-loved" items (passing the love along) but also earn the green ($)! Now that's what I call a win-win!! XO, Natalie
