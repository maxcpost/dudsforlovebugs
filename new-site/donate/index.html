Giving Back. Connecting Community.

We believe in giving back and are proud to support local non-profit organizations through our diaper drive and donation of unsold merchandise.

Our Charity Partner

All unsold donated merchandise and diapers collected during the sale are gifted to the Crisis Nursery — a vital Rochester organization that provides emergency care for children and support for families in crisis.

When you consign with us, you have the option to donate your unsold items to the Crisis Nursery rather than picking them up. It's a simple way to make a big difference.

Early Shopping Access

Two special groups get early entrance at 9 AM on Friday — a full hour before the 10 AM public opening.

Diaper Drive

Bring a new, unopened package of diapers (any size) to the sale and receive early entrance at 9 AM on Friday — one hour before the doors open to the public at 10 AM.

All diapers collected are donated directly to the Crisis Nursery to help families in need.

Military Appreciation

We want to give a special thanks to our military brothers and sisters. Show a valid military ID at the door for early entrance at 9 AM on Friday.

This is our way of saying thank you for your service and sacrifice.

How Donating Works

1

Register as a consignor

Sign up for the sale and pay the $12 registration fee. You'll use MySaleManager to tag and manage your items.

2

Check the "Donate" box when tagging

When you enter items in MySaleManager, you'll see a "Donate" checkbox for each item. Check this box for any items you'd like to go to the Crisis Nursery if they don't sell.

3

Unsold items go to a good cause

After the sale, any unsold items you marked for donation are delivered to the Crisis Nursery — no extra effort needed from you.

Register to consign and choose to donate your unsold items. Or join our mailing list to stay updated on our next sale and giving opportunities.
