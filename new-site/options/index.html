Duds for Love Bugs — Design

Three versions to compare. A Version pill at the bottom of every Bold page hops between them.

Bold Vibrant, near-neon. White hero with a dense confetti-dot field; every sub-page gets a solid-color banner with scattered white polka dots. Bold 2 Same vibrant palette as Bold, but the hero dots are one uniform size laid out in an angled grid instead of scattered confetti. Bold 3 · Spectrum Hero is a full-spectrum ombre gradient (pink → purple → blue → green → yellow → orange) with white polka dots on the angled grid. Natalie's "ombre of all the colors" idea. Bold 4 · Showcase Hero is a full-bleed, auto-rotating carousel of real event photos with the logo and countdown overlaid — Natalie's "carousel of images so people can see what the place is really like." Safe The clean, neutral version — white and charcoal with pink accents. Same layout and content.
