Volunteering at a Duds for Love Bugs Sale has its perks!

All consignors will recieve a minimum of 60% of their sales earnings.

Check out how Volunteering can help you recieve a higher percentage of your sales earnings and qualifies you for the Exclusive start time at our VIP Pre-Sale Event!

- Volunteer for 1 shift (4 hours) = 65% of your sales earnings and VIP Pre-Sale admission (5:00 PM~8:00 PM)
- Volunteer for 2 shifts (8 hours) = 70% of your sales earnings and VIP Pre-Sale admission (5:00 PM~8:00 PM)
- Volunteer for 3 shifts or more (4hrs. per shift), **1 shift must be a tear-down shift = 75% of your sales earnings and VIP Pre-Sale admission (5:00 PM~8:00 PM)
