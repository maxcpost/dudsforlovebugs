What Can I Sell?

We accept gently used children's items in "like new" or "very good" condition. If you don't "love" it, chances are no one else will "like" it either.

Accepted Items

Children's Clothing

Sizes Newborn through 14/16

Children's Shoes

Clean, odor-free, scuff-free

Toys

Clean, working, all parts included

Children's Furniture

Like-new condition

Games

Board games, puzzles, etc.

DVD Movies

Children's movies

Children's Books

Good condition

Strollers

NO car seats accepted

Beds, Bedding & Pack 'n Play

Clean, stain-free

Riding Toys

Working condition

Quality Standards

All items must be in "like new" or "very good" condition. Items that fail to meet this standard will not be eligible for sale.

Have items that are good but not quite top quality? Check out our Purple Play Rack for play clothes.

Safety First

Check the CPSC website (cpsc.gov) for any potentially recalled items before consigning. Recalled items cannot be sold.
