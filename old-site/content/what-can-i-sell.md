# What Can I Sell?

**URL:** https://dudsforlovebugs.com/what-can-i-sell/
**Title:** WHAT CAN I SELL? - Duds for Love Bugs

---

Duds for Love Bugs is a Children's Consignment Sale that will be held during the Fall/Winter and Spring/Summer seasons. Our goal is to provide our consumers with Quality childrens wear at an Affordable price! To ensure the quality of our merchandise, we ask that our consignors Only consider items that are "like new" or "very good" condition. Items that fail to meet this standard will not be eligible for sale. If you don't "love" it, chances are no one else will "like" it either!

## What can I sell?

- Children's Clothing (Sizes Newborn~14/16)
- Children's Shoes
- Children's Toys
- Children's Furniture
- Games
- DVD Movies
- Childrens Books
- Strollers (NO CAR SEATS)
- Beds and Bedding
- Pack 'n Play
- Riding Toys

**Be sure to check the CPSC website for any potentially recalled items at: www.cpsc.gov
