# Registration

**URL:** https://dudsforlovebugs.com/registration/
**Title:** REGISTRATION - Duds for Love Bugs

---

Consignors are an integral part of the success of our sales. We would like to "thank you" in advance for your participation in our event!

**All Consignors earn a minimum of 60% of their sales! (Check out Volunteering to see how to earn more!)

Consignor registration is NOW open. In order to be a consignor, you must register for each sale. Please follow the steps below:

- Pay your consignor fee of $12.00. Prior to payment, you will be required to complete a Consignor Agreement.
- All items should be brought to Drop~Off sorted by gender and size.
- Consignors will be permitted into Exclusive VIP Pre-Sale with the proper form of identification. Shopping time for those who are just consignors is Thursday, 6 PM~8 PM. Volunteers will be from 5PM~8PM.
- All unsold items will be available for pick-up on Sunday, 6 PM~7:30 PM. **Any/All items not picked up by 7:30 PM (sharp), become the property of DFLB & will be donated to a local charity.
- Consignors will receive a check for their sold items within two weeks of the conclusion of the sale.
