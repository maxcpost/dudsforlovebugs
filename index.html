About

Meet Natalie

I'm Natalie — a wife, mama of three, and passionate bargain hunter. I studied business at West Virginia University and dreamt of someday having my own.

Inspired by my very own love bugs, DFLB launched in 2016. My vision: a children's "pop-up" boutique where local families can shop quality items, clean out closets, and cash in.

I like to describe it as "going green" — recycle your gently-loved items, pass the love along, and earn the green ($). That's a win-win!

XO, Natalie
