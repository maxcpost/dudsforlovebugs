How Consigning Works

Four simple steps from sign-up to payday.

1

Register

Sign up online through MySaleManager and pay the one-time $12 consignor fee. New and returning consignors welcome.

2

Tag Your Items

Price and tag each item using our tagging guidelines. No time? Our VIP Tagging Service does it for you.

3

Drop Off

Bring your tagged items to the JCC during your scheduled drop-off time. Items are sorted by our team and displayed for sale.

4

Get Paid

Earn 60–75% of your sales. Receive your check within two weeks after the sale. Plus, shop the VIP pre-sale before the public.
