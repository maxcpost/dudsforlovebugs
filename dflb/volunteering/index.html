Volunteering Has Its Perks

All consignors earn a minimum of 60% of their sales. Volunteer your time and you'll earn even more — plus shop the VIP Pre-Sale before anyone else.

Choose Your Level

The more shifts you work, the higher your earnings percentage. Every tier includes VIP Pre-Sale access starting at 5 PM.

Why Volunteer?

Shop Before Everyone Else

Volunteers get into the VIP Pre-Sale at 5 PM — a full hour before other consignors. First pick on the best items.

Earn a Higher Percentage

Boost your earnings from 60% up to 75% of your sale price. The more you volunteer, the more you take home.

Meet Other Local Parents

Work alongside families from the Rochester area who share your love of smart shopping and keeping kids' stuff out of landfills.

Have Fun Doing It

Our volunteers love the energy of sale days. It's a feel-good way to spend a few hours while supporting your community.

Ready to Volunteer?

You must be a registered consignor before signing up for volunteer shifts. If you haven't registered yet, start there first.
