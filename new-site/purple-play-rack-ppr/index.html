The Purple Play Rack

Got play clothes that don't quite meet top-quality standards? The Purple Play Rack gives them a second chance to sell — or find a new home through donation to the Crisis Nursery.

Our Quality Mantra

“We're not a label snob, we're a quality snob!”

We don't want rips, stains, holes, or smell. We don't care where items come from, as long as they're in gently-loved condition. But for items that don't quite make the cut, we created the Purple Play Rack of play clothes.

Three Rules to Remember

Every PPR item must follow these three rules. No exceptions.

1

Maximum Price: $15.00

Nothing on the Purple Play Rack can be priced higher than $15.00.

2

Add “PPRack” to Description

When tagging, put PPRack in the item description field of the label.

3

Mark DISCOUNT and DONATE

All PPR items must be marked for both discount and donate. This is required.

How PPR Items Move Through the Sale

All PPR items will either sell or be donated. Here's the timeline.

  1. Friday & Saturday — Regular Price

    PPR items have the same opportunity to sell as every other item in the sale during the first two days at their tagged price.

  2. Sunday 10 AM – 2 PM — 50% Off

    Any remaining PPR items automatically drop to half price on Sunday morning, giving shoppers great deals on play clothes.

  3. Sunday After 2 PM — Donated

    All remaining PPR items become property of DFLB and are donated to the Crisis Nursery. Every unsold item goes to families in need.

All PPR Items Will Either Sell or Be Donated

That's the beauty of the Purple Play Rack. Your play clothes either earn you money or go directly to families who need them through the Crisis Nursery. Nothing goes to waste.
