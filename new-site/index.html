How It Works

Whether you're buying or selling, it only takes three simple steps.

For Shoppers

  1. 1

    Check the schedule

    See when the next sale opens and mark your calendar. Admission is always free.

  2. 2

    Show up and browse

    Hundreds of items organized by size and type. Clothing, toys, books, gear, furniture — everything kids need.

  3. 3

    Save big

    Take home quality items at 50-90% off retail. Sunday is half-price day for even deeper deals.

For Consignors

  1. 1

    Register online

    Sign up through MySaleManager, pay the $12 consignor fee, and schedule your drop-off time.

  2. 2

    Tag and drop off

    Price and tag your items, then bring them to the JCC during drop-off hours. No time to tag? Try our VIP Tagging Service.

  3. 3

    Get paid

    Earn 60-75% of your sales. Receive your check within two weeks. Plus, shop the VIP pre-sale before the public.

Meet Natalie

I'm Natalie — a wife, mama of three, and passionate bargain hunter. I studied business at West Virginia University and dreamt of someday having my own.

Inspired by (and affectionately named after) my very own love bugs, DFLB launched in 2016. My vision: a children's "pop-up" boutique where local families can shop quality items, clean out closets, and cash in.

I like to describe it as "going green" — you recycle your gently-loved items, passing the love along, and earn the green ($). That's what I call a win-win!

XO, Natalie
